Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much does water mitigation cost?

    The cost can vary greatly depending on the amount and category of the water. Working closely with insurance companies generally mitigation work is 100% covered by insurance!

  • How long does the mitigation process take?

    The typical timeline from when we start demolition to completely dry is 3-4 days. Some projects may take more time but most water damage can be mitigated in a short time.

  • Do I need to do anything before I call RJM?

    No, as soon as you know of water damage please contact RJM and our professionals can walk you through the entire process.

  • Can RJM finish a mitigation job that was already started?

    Yes, RJM can take over and complete a mitigation job that was started by another company. We are well-versed in project take over and finishing mitigation projects that have been troublesome.

  • How does RJM determine what is affected?

    We use state of the art thermal imaging cameras, infrared detectors, and moisture meters to determine not only the affected areas but also throughout the mitigation process to determine humidity and temperatures. This coupled with our top of the line equipment ensures the fastest dry time with the least amount of hassle for our clients.
